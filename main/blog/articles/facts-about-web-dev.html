5 Facts Ever Small Business Owners Needs To Know About Web Design

OCTOBER 21, 2020

88% of consumers who search for a type of business on a mobile device call or go to that business within 24 hours

This means having your SEO dialed in is very important. Not only do you want users to be able to find your site but, you want them to click onto it as well... and enjoy the process all at the same time

83% of mobile users say that a seamless experience across all devices is very important

94%of negative website feedback was design related.

70%of small business websites lack a Call to Action (CTA) on their homepage

A call to action is VERY easy to implement. It can be as simple as setting up a news letter sign up on the page or inviting them to follow you on one of your social media pages.

75%of consumers admit to making judgements on a company’s credibility based on the company’s website design
